How do I install this app?

To install this app, follow the directions for your browser.

For Safari on iOS, press the "share" icon on the toolbar at the top. Scroll through the bottom row of the popup until you find "Add to Home Screen".

For Chrome, Chromium Browsers, or Firefox, press the settings icon on the bottom right of the drive tracker app. If you see "Install App" drive tracker can be installed click "Install App" and you will be prompted to add the app to your home screen.

The app is now available offline

Who is this app designed for?

This app is designed to be on either the student phone or the parent phone. It's designed to be not distracting for student phones, and has helpful features like a speedometer for a parent phone.

How do I print the log?

Press the print button at the bottom of the screen. A print dialog will appear. On iOS, only Airprint printers are supported.

I have issues with the app.

You can submit issues or even fix them yourself here. This app is a one person project created by an actual student driver, so don't expect a speedy response.

What if I get a new phone?

If you get a new phone, your data might not be transferred to the new device. Be sure to export your data in settings and send the file you downloaded to your new phone. On the new device, open the setup screen for driving log, press the import data option at the bottom of the screen. A popup will show. Upload the JSON file and press Import. Finish setting up driving log and see your data on the home screen.

Can I get this on my phone's app store?

If this app gets enough users, this app can be uploaded to the Play Store. Apple's App Store doesn't support PWAs at the moment, but adding it to your home screen downloads it and adds it to the home screen, just like a normal app.

Exporting your Data

On Android or your Computer, press the share button, then export your data. A JSON file will be downloaded

iOS is a little more complicated due to issues with Safari not supporting standard HTML practices. On iOS 13.4 or later, press share at the bottom of your screen and press Export Data. Some text will be copied to your clipboard. In order to save it, paste it into a new note.

On older versions of iOS, when you export your data you'll be shown some text. Press and hold on the screen and press "Select All", now save it in a new note.
